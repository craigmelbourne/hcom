Location.
Located in London (Kings Cross - St. Pancras), Great Northern Hotel is close to British Library, University of London, and King's College London. Also nearby are London Zoo and Trafalgar Square.

Hotel Features.
The hotel offers 3 restaurants along with a bar/lounge. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available in public areas. There is a business center on site. Additional amenities include tour/ticket assistance, a snack bar/deli, and room service (during limited hours).

Guestrooms.
The 91 air-conditioned guestrooms at Great Northern Hotel include safes and complimentary newspapers. Guests can use the in-room complimentary wireless high-speed Internet access. 40-inch flat-screen TVs are equipped with satellite channels and first-run movies. Bathrooms offer showers with rainfall showerheads, bathrobes, hair dryers, and complimentary toiletries. In-room refrigerators and complimentary bottled water are provided. In addition, a turndown service is offered nightly, housekeeping is available daily, and amenities available on request include irons/ironing boards.
