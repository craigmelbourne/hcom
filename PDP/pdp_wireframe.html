Luxury London hotel at St. Pancras International

Steps from St. Pancras
The luxe St. Pancras Renaissance London Hotel blends Old World opulence and modern hospitality. You'll be in an extravagantly designed 19th-century Victorian building attached to St. Pancras International, London's Eurostar terminal.

A transportation hub
Steps from your guestroom, you can hop a train, bus, taxi, or Tube to almost anywhere in London â€“ not to mention Paris and Brussels. Covent Garden with its array of pubs, restaurants, and covered markets is just a couple miles away.

Old building, new perks
Beyond the hotel's thrilling neo-gothic facade, a range of modern amenities await. The 3 bars and restaurants include The Gilbert Scott, operated by Marcus Wareing. Feeling indulgent? Personal butler service is available, or you can get your nails polished in the onsite spa.

Contemporary comforts
Lavish guestrooms mix original touches like high ceilings and expansive windows with contemporary comfort: think Egyptian-cotton linens, pillowtop mattresses, and flat-screen TVs. WiFi is available, and room service never sleeps.
